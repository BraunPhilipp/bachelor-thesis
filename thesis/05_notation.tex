$S$ & stride or step size \\

$\epsilon$ & perturbation constraint \\

$\bm{e}_k$ & canonical vector \\
$\bm{v}_{max}$ & eigenvector with maximum eigenvalue \\
$\mcl{S}$ & subset for a single pixel \\
$Z$ & input image depth \\
